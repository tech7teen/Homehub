Frequently Asked Questions

How do I pay rent on HomeHub? ▼
We support M-Pesa and Bank Transfer. Simply select your preferred method, enter the amount, and complete the transaction. It takes less than a minute!
Is HomeHub secure? ▼
Yes! We use bank-level encryption to protect all your data and transactions. Your information is always safe with us.
Can I get a receipt for my payment? ▼
Absolutely! You'll receive a receipt immediately after payment, and you can access all your past receipts in your payment history.
How long does payment processing take? ▼
M-Pesa payments are processed instantly. Bank transfers typically take 1-2 business days depending on your bank.
